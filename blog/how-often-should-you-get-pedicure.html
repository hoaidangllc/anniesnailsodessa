How Often Should You Get a Pedicure

Many people enjoy pedicures for beauty and relaxation, but regular pedicures also help keep feet clean, healthy, and comfortable. A common question is how often you should get a pedicure to maintain the best results.

At Annie's Nails & Spa, we recommend a regular schedule based on your lifestyle, activity level, and foot care needs.

Recommended Pedicure Schedule

For most people, getting a pedicure every 3 to 4 weeks is a good routine. This helps keep toenails neat, skin smooth, and feet feeling fresh.

  • Every 3 to 4 weeks for regular maintenance
  • More often during sandal season or summer months
  • As needed for dry skin or callus care

Benefits of Regular Pedicures

Pedicures are not only about appearance. They also support foot hygiene and comfort.

  • Helps prevent rough skin buildup
  • Keeps toenails trimmed and shaped
  • Improves the appearance of your feet
  • Provides a relaxing self care experience
